The Elite

The jersey that started Team Elite's journey to greatness. Worn with pride during the rise of one of India's most dominant Free Fire squads, it represents discipline, teamwork, and the championship-winning spirit that defined the team's legacy.

The Blue Army jersey symbolizes the unwavering support of Team Elite's community and the fearless mindset of its players. Inspired by the team's relentless pursuit of victory, it stands for passion, unity, and the determination to compete at the highest level.
